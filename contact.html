Emma Raymaekers is a graphic designer based in Brussels. She works as a freelance graphic designer, focusing on collaborations in music, art and literature and is part of Twaalfmaal.
In her work, Emma focuses on the intuitive play of the mind. She enjoyes exploring this intuition and tries to follow her visual gut feeling as closely as possible.
In 2020 she graduated at KASK School of Arts Ghent with a master degree in the visual arts / graphic design. She did her internship at Het Balanseer publisher and was part of HS71 collective, Bebe Books and Parlor.
